Spotify is a music, podcast and video streaming service launched in 2008. It currently has approximately
140 million users and providing over 30 million songs. Spotify uses cloud services in very clever and
interesting way, in my opinion. Spotify leverages AWS which allows Spotify to regularly evaluate its
infrastructure in order to meet continously changing business needs and ambitions.

Why use Amazon Web Services?

Emil Fredriksson, operations director for Spotify, expresses, that "Spotify needed a storage solution
that could easy scale very quickly without incurring long lead times for upgrades". Leveraging AWS
enables orgainations to ability to scale applications as the business grows. Before obtaining new storage
could take several months, it can now be accquired immedately by using AWS.
